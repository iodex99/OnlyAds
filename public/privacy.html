Privacy Protocols

ONLYADS.ME // SYSTEM LOG

1. The Philosophy of Data

OnlyAds is an experimental project exploring the attention economy. Unlike other websites that hide their tracking, we visualize it. By using this site, you acknowledge that you are participating in the open web's digital advertising ecosystem.

2. Data Collection & Cookies

We use Google AdSense to serve targeted advertisements. Google and its partners use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website or other websites on the internet.

  • Third-Party Vendors: Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits.
  • Advertising Cookies: Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to OnlyAds.me and/or other sites on the Internet.

3. How Ads Target Users

This website demonstrates browser fingerprinting. We utilize standard browser APIs (Navigator, Screen, Network Information) to display the data that is broadcast to ad exchanges during Real-Time Bidding (RTB) auctions. We do not store this data on our own servers; it is displayed locally on your device for educational and satirical purposes.

4. Opting Out

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. Alternatively, you can opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting www.aboutads.info.

5. Contact

For inquiries regarding this art project, contact us at: onlyads.me@rediffmail.com
